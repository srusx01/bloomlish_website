Kicked off in a 2025 at uniska (universitas islam kalimantan) as a little space where we could grow together through English. For three months, we shared laughs, struggled with words, messed up grammar, and slowly built the courage to actually speak up..

Behind Bloomlish is ms bella, ms aisha and sir haikal who started this whole thing with one simple goal: to help friends at Uniska practice English without fear, and prove that learning together is way more fun than doing it alone.
